Effective Date: August 2, 2025

What information do we collect?
Centris Scraper Pro does not collect any personal information or data about its users. The extension only reads publicly available property listing data from centris.ca to provide scraping functionality. We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data, browsing history, or persistent identifiers. No server or HTTP logs are generated or retained by the extension.

How do we use the information?
Centris Scraper Pro uses the data read from centris.ca solely to make property listing information available to the user through the scraper tool. This data is processed locally within your browser and is not transmitted to us or any third party. We do not use any information for analytics, advertising, or user recognition purposes.

What information do we share?
Centris Scraper Pro does not share any information with third parties under any circumstances. No user data, scraped data, or usage information is transmitted, sold, or disclosed to any external entities.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the extension's support page.
